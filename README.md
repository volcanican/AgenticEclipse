# AgenticEclipse
Video Analysis platform
Short Description:
Agentic Eclispe is intelligent video ananlysis platform that breaks the provided video into frames and analyzes each frame then compiles them understanding each frame leads it to get a full view of the entire video and give summary according to it

Long description:
We are living in a time where there is a lot of video data. The tools we use to look at videos are not working well because they are slow and do a lot of extra work. They also do not really understand what is going on in the videos. Agentic Eclipse is trying to fix this problem. It is using a way of working that is, like a team of agents. This was made for the AMD Developer Hackathon.

The main new idea is something we call the 'Storyboard Strategy'. This makes it easier to look at videos by choosing the important parts using OpenCV. It also helps us not waste the keys we need to use the video tools.. It makes sure the story of the video still makes sense. The part of the system that works in the background is controlled by Python and the Fireworks API. This lets users get reports that tell them what they want to know about the videos. They can even choose how they want these reports to sound.
